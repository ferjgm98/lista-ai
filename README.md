# ListaAI 🚀

**AI-powered product listing content for Mercado Libre sellers.**

Upload product photos → Get optimized titles, descriptions, and images in Spanish and Portuguese. Ready to publish.

## The Problem

5M+ Mercado Libre sellers across 18 LATAM countries spend hours writing product listings. Most aren't marketers — they're boutique owners, resellers, and manufacturers with great products but bad listings. Meanwhile, listing quality directly impacts ML's search ranking and conversion rates.

## The Solution

ListaAI turns raw product photos into complete, search-optimized listings in 30 seconds.

1. Upload 1-5 product photos
2. AI generates: title, short desc, long SEO desc, category, keywords, enhanced photos
3. Review, edit, and publish directly to Mercado Libre

## Stack

- **Backend:** Bun + Hono
- **Frontend:** React 19 + Vite
- **Database:** SQLite (Bun:sqlite)
- **AI:** GPT-4o / Claude Sonnet 4
- **Images:** Replicate / Stability AI
- **Auth:** JWT
- **Payments:** Stripe
- **Hosting:** Vercel + Cloudflare
